Writer. Illustrator. Filmmaker. Actor. Singer. Jeremiah Chiedza is a Zambian multi-talented creative who builds in silence and releases with intention.

He founded Emberline Studios Limited with a vision rooted in Shonen Jump's spirit, a full creative studio that protects artists, distributes their work, and builds African stories for a global audience.

"The difference between me and future me is just knowledge and the application of it."
